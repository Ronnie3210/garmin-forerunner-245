How to Install the Forerunner 245 Update

Step-by-Step Update Guide

  1. Charge Your Watch: Ensure your Forerunner 245 has at least 50% battery before starting.
  2. Connect to Garmin Connect: Open the Garmin Connect app on your paired smartphone.
  3. Check for Updates: Go to More > Settings > Device > System Update.
  4. Download the Update: If available, select "Install Update" to begin downloading.
  5. Transfer to Your Watch: Keep your phone near your watch during transfer (takes 10-15 minutes).
  6. Complete Installation: The watch will install automatically - don't press buttons during this process.
  7. Restart Your Watch: After installation, your Forerunner 245 may restart to finalize changes.

Alternative Method: Update via Garmin Express on your computer by connecting your watch with USB cable.
